Short Bio

Kingsley Lei Shi is a first year graduate student at Institute of Software, the Chinese Academy of Sciences(CAS). Before joining CAS, He got his bachelor degree in computer science from Wuhan University in 2012.

He is working as a research intern (since Apr, 2013) at Knowledge Mining group, Microsoft Research Aisa, supervised by Dr. Shuming Shi. He also worked as a SDE intern (from Nov, 2011 to Apr, 2012) at Search Technology Center, Microsoft.

CV will be available in a few days.

Research Interests

His research interests include Data Mining, Machine Learning, Recommender System and Information Extraction. Currently, He focuses on text mining, approximate k-nn Graph construction and tensor factorization.

Research & Development Experiences

Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences(Sep,2012-Present, Beijing, China)
Research Assitant
I mainly work on the topic of fast k-NN graph construction. k-NN graphs are widely used in several areas, such as data mining, machine learning and recommender systems. Our goal of this work is to generate k-NN graph with a high precision and reduced time cost.

Microsoft Research Asia (Apr,2013-Present, Beijing, China)
Research Intern
I am with the knowledge mining group, supervised by Dr. Shuming Shi. During the internship, I mainly focus on short text understanding.

STCA, Microsoft (Nov,2011-Apr,2012, Beijing, China)
Software Development Intern
I worked with the multi media team, search technology center Asia (STCA), mentored by Dr. Litian Tao. During the internship, I focus on improving the search relevance of Bing in image verticle.

Institute of Computer Science, Wuhan University (June,2010-June,2012, Wuhan, China)
Research Assistant
I worked with Prof. Wenbin Hu on the topics about self-adaptive and intelligent strategy for yard space plan under uncertainty. I mainly focused on the stowage plan which can be formalized as a combinatorial problem. Container classification algorithm and container stacking strategy are proposed, and group-based encoding genetic algorithm is used to solve this problem.
