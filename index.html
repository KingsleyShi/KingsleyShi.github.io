Short Bio

Lei Shi is a first year graduate student at Institute of Software, the Chinese Academy of Sciences. Before joining CAS, He got his bachelor degree from Wuhan University, China.

He is working as a research intern (since Apr, 2013) at Knowledge Mining group, Microsoft Research Aisa, supervised by Dr. Shuming Shi. He also worked as a SDE intern (from Nov, 2011 to Apr, 2012) at Search Technology Center, Microsoft. Here is his CV.
