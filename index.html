Short Bio

Lei Shi is a second year graduate student at Institute of Software, the Chinese Academy of Sciences(CAS). Before going to CAS, He got his bachelor degree in computer science from Wuhan University in 2012. He worked as a research intern (from Apr, 2013 to Jan, 2014) in Knowledge Mining group at Microsoft Research Asia. He also worked as a SDE intern (from Nov, 2011 to Apr, 2012) at Search Technology Center, Microsoft. His research interests are machine learning and its applications in data mining and natural language processing. Currently, he mainly works on feature selection and short text understanding.
